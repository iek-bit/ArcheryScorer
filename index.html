Achery Scorer

Version 2.2.11

Achery Scorer is a mobile-first archery scoring app for tracking practice and tournament sessions, reviewing session history, and analyzing arrow grouping over time.

Highlights
Manual and tournament scoring, saved session history, true grouping heatmaps, theme customization, and location-aware session logging.

Help Guide

Scoring Basics

To score an arrow, tap directly on the target exactly where your real arrow landed. The app calculates the ring score automatically.

  • Magnify Tool: Tap and hold on the target to pop up a magnifying lens. Drag your finger to aim the crosshair precisely, then release to place the arrow.
  • Misses: If you miss the target completely, tap the "M" button in the top right corner.
  • Undo: Use the undo button in the top navigation to remove the last placed arrow.

Tournaments vs Practice

Practice: You have full control. Change target faces and distances at any time between rounds. Sessions can be saved at any length.

Tournaments: Follow a strict 6-round sequence. The target face and distance change automatically depending on your starting animal and direction.

Quick Score: Useful for a fast round you don't care to save to your history.

Stats & Visuals

Your stats aggregate based on your currently active filters. Use the "Location" button to only view stats from specific ranges.

  • True Heatmap: A visual mapping of where your arrows tend to group. Deep red indicates your tightest clusters, while blue indicates sparse outliers. Pinch to zoom and drag to pan the heatmap.
  • Timeline: Pinch to zoom and pan along the timeline. Tap on specific points to see the date and score of that exact session.

Data Management

Your sessions are automatically synced to the club server when you log in. On a new device, simply log in with your account to restore your full history.
